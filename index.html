🔰 Futures Trading એટલે શું?

1
Contract:

Future date ના price પર Buy/Sell કરવાનો contract. ખરેખર crypto ખરીદ્યા વગર profit/loss થાય.

2
Leverage:

ઓછા પૈસા વડે મોટો trade. ₹1000 ના 10x leverage = ₹10,000 નો position. ફાયદો અને નુકસાન બંને 10 ગણા.

3
Long vs Short:

Long = price વધશે એવું માનીને Buy. Short = price ઘટશે એવું માનીને Sell.

📐 Support & Resistance Strategy

✅ Beginner માટે Best – Simple અને Effective

1
Support identify:

Price bar-bar જ્યાંથી bounce થઈ ઊભો, તે Support level.

2
Entry (Long):

Price support ટચ કરી bullish candle close → Buy entry. Stop Loss: support ની 1-2% નીચે.

3
Entry (Short):

Price resistance ટચ કરી bearish candle → Sell entry. Stop Loss: resistance ની 1-2% ઉપર.

4
Exit:

Target = Support-Resistance ના range ના 70-80%. RSI 70+ = Exit signal.

📈 Trend Following Strategy

✅ Strong Market Moves (Bull/Bear) – Medium term

1
Trend confirm:

EMA 20 > EMA 50 = Uptrend. EMA 20 < EMA 50 = Downtrend. (1hr chart use કરો)

2
Entry:

Uptrend: Price EMA 20 ને touch કરી ઉપર bounce = Long entry. Downtrend: Reverse.

3
Stop Loss:

EMA 50 ની નીચે/ઉપર (Uptrend/Downtrend)

4
Exit:

EMA 20 cross EMA 50 opposite side = Exit. અથવા previous high/low break.

⚡ Scalping Strategy

⚠️ Advanced – Fast moves, 5-15 min chart. નવા traders ટાળો.

1
Best Time:

6:30 PM – 9:00 PM IST (high volume). 5min chart use.

2
Entry:

Momentum candle + Volume spike + RSI 50 cross → Quick entry.

3
Stop Loss:

Tight – 0.3% to 0.5% only.

4
Exit:

Target: 0.5–1% profit. Maximum 15-30 min hold. Time limit ઓળંગ્યે Exit.

📝 Trade Journal – Daily Record

દરરોજ આ note down કરો:

📌 Entry price, Exit price, Stop Loss

📌 Reason for trade, Indicator used

📌 Profit/Loss amount

📌 What went right / What went wrong

✅ Journal = Best teacher. 30 days track = Pattern visible.

⚠️ Common ભૂલો – ટાળો

❌ Stop Loss ન મૂકવો – Loss unlimited થઈ જાય, account wipe.

❌ High Leverage (20x-100x) – Beginner ક્યારેય નહીં. 1 candle ≠ liquidation.

❌ FOMO Trade – Price ઝડપથી ઉપર ગઈ = Panic buy. Almost always loss.

❌ Revenge Trade – Loss પછી ગુસ્સામાં trade = Double loss.

❌ Over-trading – ઘણા trades = Fees + Stress + Loss.

❌ News time Overexposure – CPI, FOMC, Fed news = Extreme volatile. New trader: avoid position.

⚠️ Unverified Signals – Telegram/YouTube "guru" signals follow ન કરો.

⚠️ Weekend ઓછો Volume – Saturday/Sunday: low liquidity, choppy. Careful.
